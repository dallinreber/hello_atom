My name is Dallin Reber, and I am the potter behind Clay by Dallin. I started my adventure in pottery in ceramics my sophomore year of high school, and I have been in love ever since. I started selling my work at art shows my junior year, and then built an Etsy shop when a senior. Since then, I have tried to keep creating as much as possible, and I now work as an instructor at the Kreative Kiln in Pleasant Grove, UT.

I enjoy doing anything outdoors, including:

  • Running
  • Hiking
  • Swimming
  • And anything with wildlife!
