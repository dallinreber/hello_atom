My Process:

Most of my work is started on the potters' wheel, but I almost always add some sort of hand-building or hand-sculpting to the piece before calling it done. I fire to cone 5, and I try to make sure that all of my functional pieces are food safe.

From start to finish, the steps for making one of my mugs goes as follows:

  • Wedging to Remove Airbubbles
  • Throwing on the Wheel
  • Dry to Leather-hard
  • Trimming a Foot
  • Handles and Sculpting
  • Reaching Bone Dry
  • Into the Fire
